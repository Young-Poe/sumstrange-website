Who We Are

SUMSTRANGE is a creative collective built on authenticity, resilience, and frequency. Born in Houston, forged through sound, sweat, and shared hunger—we’re artists, engineers, visionaries, and disruptors who build from the underground up.

From studio sessions to stage takeovers, every piece of what we do is intentional. Whether it's helping artists find their voice, curating community-driven events, or crafting records that punch through the noise—we move with purpose.

We don’t just rent you time. We help you create timelines.

The Mission

Stay independent. Stay human. Stay heard. Everything we build—from our gear to our grants—is designed to amplify real voices and reshape what it means to be a collective in 2025 and beyond.
